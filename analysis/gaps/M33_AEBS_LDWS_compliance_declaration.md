# M33 — Formal AEBS + LDWS/LDPS Compliance Declaration

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Risk Level** | Medium (Traceability / Documentation Gap) |
| **EU Reference** | UN R.171 S2 §5.1.5 |
| **CN Reference** | GB draft §4.1.16 |
| **Gap Type** | Traceability / documentation gap |

> CN GB contains an equivalent requirement. Gap is formal compliance declaration for EU type approval submission.

---

## EU Requirement (R.171 §5.1.5)

> A vehicle equipped with DCAS shall also be equipped with:
> - An AEBS (Automatic Emergency Braking System) meeting the requirements of UN R.131
> - An LDWS (Lane Departure Warning System) or LDPS (Lane Departure Prevention System) meeting the requirements of UN R.130

The manufacturer must provide formal evidence (type approval certificates) that the AEBS and LDWS/LDPS on the DCAS-equipped vehicle meet these regulations.

---

## China L2GB Status

GB draft §4.1.16 states:

> 搭载本标准所规定系统的车辆，还应符合GB/T 39323（自动紧急制动系统）和GB/T 41798（车道偏离预警系统）的相关要求。

Translation: Vehicles equipped with the system regulated by this standard shall also comply with the relevant requirements of GB/T 39323 (Automatic Emergency Braking System) and GB/T 41798 (Lane Departure Warning System).

**CN and EU are aligned in substance** — both require AEBS and LDWS/LDPS as baseline systems on DCAS-equipped vehicles. CN references the equivalent national standards (GB/T 39323 for AEBS, GB/T 41798 for LDWS).

---

## Key Documentation Requirement

For EU type approval under R.171, the submission must include the actual type approval certificates for:
1. AEBS under UN R.131 (not just GB/T 39323 — EU requires the UN Regulation)
2. LDWS/LDPS under UN R.130 (not just GB/T 41798)

G1.3's CN AEBS and LDWS approvals under GB standards may not be accepted as direct equivalents for EU type approval. The EU TA will expect to see UN R.131 and UN R.130 certificates.

---

## Required Closure Actions

1. Confirm whether G1.3 AEBS is already type-approved under UN R.131 (for EU markets) in addition to GB/T 39323 (for CN market)
2. Confirm whether G1.3 LDWS/LDPS is already type-approved under UN R.130 in addition to GB/T 41798
3. If UN R.131/R.130 approvals do not exist: add to the EU type approval project scope as prerequisite approvals before R.171 DCAS approval
4. Document the AEBS and LDWS/LDPS approval numbers in the R.171 DCAS type approval submission
5. Confirm G1.3 AEBS covers VRU (pedestrian, cyclist) detection as required by UN R.131 Amendment 2+
