# R79-1 — Lane Change Lateral Acceleration ≤ 1 m/s² Beyond Curvature

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Risk Level** | Medium-High (R.79 stricter than R.171 — binding on Cat C) |
| **R.79 Reference** | UN R.79 04 S6 §5.6.4.4(a) |
| **R.171 Reference** | §6.2.3 (1.5 m/s²) |
| **CN Reference** | GB draft §4.6.2.2.1.9 (1.5 m/s², straight road only) |
| **Gap Type** | R.79 stricter than R.171 and L2GB — both apply, take stricter |

> This is the most significant R.79-specific gap. R.79 §5.6.4.4 applies directly to Cat C (driver-confirmed lane change) and sets a stricter limit than R.171 §6.2.3. Where both regulations apply, the stricter value governs.

---

## R.79 Requirement (§5.6.4.4)

> The lateral acceleration induced by the system during the lane change manoeuvre:
> (a) Shall not exceed **1 m/s²** in addition to the lateral acceleration generated by the lane curvature, and
> (b) Shall not cause the total vehicle lateral acceleration to exceed the maximum values in Table 1 (§5.6.2.1.3).

The 1 m/s² "beyond curvature" limit is the delta between the curvature-induced lateral acceleration and the total lateral acceleration during the lane change.

---

## R.171 / CN L2GB Status

**R.171 §6.2.3**: lateral acceleration beyond curvature ≤ **1.5 m/s²** — less strict than R.79.

**CN GB §4.6.2.2.1.9**: on straight roads, lateral acceleration ≤ **1.5 m/s²** — same value as R.171, and restricted to straight roads only.

The current M7 gap analysis identified the EU/CN straight-road alignment and the curved-road gap. However, M7 referenced R.171's 1.5 m/s² limit. **R.79 §5.6.4.4 applies to Cat C lane changes regardless of road curvature and sets 1 m/s², not 1.5 m/s².**

---

## Applicability to G1.3

G1.3 DCAS includes a driver-confirmed lane change function. This is ACSF Category C under R.79 §5.6.4. As a Cat C function, §5.6.4.4 applies directly. The driver-confirmed lane change simultaneously falls under:
- R.79 §5.6.4.4: ≤ 1 m/s² beyond curvature
- R.171 §6.2.3: ≤ 1.5 m/s² beyond curvature

Both regulations apply. The stricter limit (1 m/s²) governs for EU type approval.

**Summary of binding limits for G1.3 EU driver-confirmed lane change:**

| Limit | R.79 §5.6.4.4 | R.171 §6.2.3 | CN §4.6.2.2.1.9 | Binding |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Beyond-curvature lateral accel | **1.0 m/s²** | 1.5 m/s² | 1.5 m/s² (straight) | **1.0 m/s²** |
| Total lateral accel | ≤ aysmax (Table 1, max 3 m/s²) | ≤ 3.5 m/s² | ≤ 3.5 m/s² | **3.0 m/s²** |
| Lateral jerk (moving avg 0.5s) | Table 1 | ≤ 5 m/s³ | ≤ 5 m/s³ | ≤ 5 m/s³ |
