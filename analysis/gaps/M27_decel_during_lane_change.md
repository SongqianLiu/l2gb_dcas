## EU Requirement (R.171 §6.2.4.3)

> During a lane change maneuver, system-commanded deceleration shall not exceed 2 m/s².

This ensures that a lane change maneuver does not impose sudden hard braking that could destabilize the vehicle or surprise following traffic.

---

## China L2GB Status

GB draft §4.6.2.2.1.11 states:

> 系统在换道过程中产生的纵向减速度不应超过2 m/s²。

Translation: The longitudinal deceleration generated by the system during a lane change shall not exceed 2 m/s².

**CN and EU are identical** — both set the same threshold of ≤2 m/s² for system-commanded longitudinal deceleration during lane changes.
