# M30 — Annual Reporting to Type Approval Authority

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Risk Level** | Medium (Missing in CN — EU-specific regulatory obligation) |

| **Gap Type** | Missing in CN (EU-specific post-market surveillance) |

---

## EU Requirement (R.171 §7.2.3)

> The manufacturer shall submit an annual report to the Type Approval Authority summarizing:
> - The number of vehicles in service with the approved DCAS
> - Any safety-critical occurrences reported under §7.2.1
> - Software updates issued and their scope
> - Any identified systematic issues or trends

This is an ongoing post-market reporting obligation, distinct from the incident-level reporting in §7.2.1.

---

## China L2GB Status

The CN GB draft does not contain an equivalent annual reporting obligation for L2GB DCAS manufacturers.

China's post-market vehicle safety monitoring operates through the **NHTSA-equivalent CATARC/MIIT** defect investigation system, but this is not a manufacturer-initiated annual reporting obligation specific to DCAS.

**This specific annual reporting requirement is not found in the CN L2GB standard.** It is an EU-specific post-market surveillance mechanism.

---

## Required Closure Actions

1. Identify the EU TA that will receive annual R.171 reports for G1.3
2. Design the annual reporting template: fleet size, incidents, software versions, systematic issues
3. Establish data infrastructure to generate annual reports: vehicle count by software version, incident count, DCAS software update history
4. Assign organizational responsibility (regulatory affairs team) for annual reporting
5. Set up an internal calendar: annual reports are typically due within Q1 of the following year
6. Document the annual reporting commitment in the EU type approval submission
