L2GB ADRS - 产品说明​
This article presents the product description of L2GB ADRS, including requirements from GB7258 and details about system usage instructions and product specifications. Key points include:​
Chinese requirement in product manual: All text in the motor vehicle's product instruction manual should be in Chinese according to GB7258 4.16.14.​
System usage instruction provision: Vehicle manufacturers with combined driving - assistance systems should provide system usage instructions publicly, such as via official websites, in - vehicle displays, or mobile devices.​
Content of system usage instruction: It should be concise and cover aspects like system category, driver's responsibility, correct usage, system's assistance mode, capabilities and limitations, ODD boundary, system states, and more.​
Product specification details: According to GB7258 4.16.13, it should include the number and installation location of sensors like cameras and radars, and the data extraction method of the combined driving - assistance data recording system.​
Driver - related information: Information about driver's responsibility, correct usage, and driver -脱离 detection (including detection methods and warning signals) should be provided.​
Interaction and emergency: Details about human - machine interaction (activation, exit, and status prompts) and emergency handling in case of accidents are required.​
Risk and compatibility: For multi - user management and vehicles with trailer - compatible systems, relevant risk descriptions and compatibility requirements should be provided. ​
