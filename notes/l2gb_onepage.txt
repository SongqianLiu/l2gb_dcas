L2GB Onepage​
This article presents information about the L2GB Onepage project, including target versions, engineering and final versions, target platforms/models, vehicle model projects, version strategies, and various requirements and their associated Jira links. Key points include: ​
Versions and Platforms：The target version is NT2 collinear, with engineering version BL350 and final version BL360. NT3.5 is to be implemented. Target platforms/models include NT3.5 P1+Lidar, Pisces ET5T, and Qinlin L50.​
Vehicle Model Projects：There are multiple vehicle model projects, such as Cetus G1.F, Pisces - leading, Qinling - leading, etc., with different申报方式 (declaration methods), SUD times, HOMO times, AD prehomo times, AD versions, L2GB exam times, and detection institutions.​
Version Strategies：There are version strategies with sync blocks and external domain dependencies. Pisces L2GB has new regulations and strong standards with AD prehomo in 2026.9.1, and Qinling L2GB HOMO Leading has AD prehomo in 2026.9.20.​
Exam Schemes：There are exam schemes for different models like Qinling, Pisces, Hercules, etc., with different options.​
Requirements and Jira Links：There are various L2GB ADRS requirements such as system activation, system exit, driver motion control intervention, etc., each with an associated Jira link and a person in charge.​
Visual Fusion Speed Limit Scheme：There is a visual fusion speed limit scheme SAS2 with a related L2GB ADRS - Speed Limit requirement and Jira link.​
Other Requirements：There are also requirements for ODC, TLD, HMI, product description, etc., each with its own Jira link and person in charge. ​
